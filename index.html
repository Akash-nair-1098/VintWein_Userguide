USER GUIDE

1) Download VintWein App from iPhone’s App Store, Android’s Google Play Store.

2) To open the app, tap the vintwein icon on your home screen.


3) Splash Screen and Home Screen are shown above.

4) Click on the Scan button to scan the wine label. Make sure to allow vintwein access to your camera in your settings.

5) Take a photo the wine label(as shown in image below).

6) After previewing the photo, click the button. To retake the photo, click the button.

7) The loading progress is shown after clicking on the tick button in camera screen(as in image below).


8) Now you can view the name, vintage and country of the scanned wine(as shown below).

9) To buy the wine, click the option at the bottom of the screen.

10) Once you click the buy button, you will be directed to an online wine store from where you can buy the wine.
